🚀 vSphere Disk Expansion & LVM Growth Procedure

Step 1: Expand the Existing Disk in vSphere

Increase the virtual disk size from the VMware vSphere Console before extending Linux storage.

Step 2: Verify Disk Size
fdisk -l /dev/sdb
Step 3: Resize Physical Volume
pvresize -dv /dev/sdb
Step 4: Verify Volume Group Free Space
vgs
Step 5: Extend Logical Volume
lvextend -L +500G /dev/vg_data_01/lv_pulpdata_01
Step 6: Grow XFS Filesystem
xfs_growfs /dev/mapper/vg_data_01-lv_pulpdata_01
Step 7: Verify Updated Capacity
df -h
